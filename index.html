About PRaSUM

PRaSUM is a technology-driven startup committed to delivering cutting-edge, scalable, and innovative digital solutions across diverse domains including Artificial Intelligence and DeepTech, Wireless Telecommunications, Educational Technology, Smart Industrial Automation and Banking Solutions.

Our Vision

To become a globally trusted technology company, delivering intelligent deep-tech solutions that transform rural India's learning landscape, drive digital transformation for MSME industries, provide advanced banking and financial digital solutions and advance wireless technologies for modern applications.

Our Mission

To innovate relentlessly, deliver excellence, and empower businesses through transformative technology, while fostering a culture of integrity, collaboration and continuous learning.
